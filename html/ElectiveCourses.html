Elective courses

Here you will find the resources for the elective courses required within the credit hours of the academic plan. In the first year, it is recommended to take the IC course along with Phys201. In the second year, it is recommended to take two IC courses with Math254 and Oper122. The final year is dedicated only to the department requirements.
